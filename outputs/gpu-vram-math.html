GPU VRAM Math for LLMs

A quick estimator for whether a model likely fits in GPU memory. Weight precision gives the base cost, but KV cache, batching, and framework overhead are the taxes that usually surprise people.

Rule of Thumb
VRAM ≈ parameters(B) × (bits/8) + overhead + KV cache

// rough planning tool, not a runtime guarantee
Caveat
GGUF and other quantized runtimes are runtime-specific. “Fits in 6 GB” can be true in one stack and false in another if dequantization or extra buffers are involved.
